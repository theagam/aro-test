About Us

Our Journey

Aromatic Fresh Kenya Limited was founded with a vision to revolutionize the agribusiness landscape in Kenya. Established in 2020, our journey began on a 10-acre production site in the vibrant town of Kitengela, Kajiado county, Kenya. With a commitment to quality and sustainability, we have quickly positioned ourselves as a promising player in the herb sector. Our founders, driven by a passion for agriculture and a desire to provide fresh, high-quality herbs, embarked on this venture to meet the growing demand for aromatic herbs internationally.

In our short time since inception, Aromatic Fresh Kenya Limited has made significant strides in the agribusiness sector. We have successfully established our production site, implemented sustainable farming practices, and built a growing customer base. Our commitment to quality has earned us recognition in the international market, and we are poised for further growth as we expand our reach globally.

Our team is the heart of Aromatic Fresh Kenya Limited. Comprising skilled and passionate individuals, we work collaboratively to ensure the highest standards of quality in our herb production. Each team member brings unique expertise and a shared commitment to excellence. Together, we foster a culture of innovation and sustainability, ensuring that our practices not only benefit our customers but also contribute positively to the environment and the community.

Our Vision

To lead the region in the production and export of high-quality herbs and be known for our steadfast dedication to sustainability, innovation, and quality. Our goal is to create a vibrant ecosystem that supports our farmers, encourages environmental responsibility, and establishes the benchmark for quality in all facets of herb production, from seed to shelf.

Our Mission

To cultivate and deliver the finest quality fresh cut herbs from the heart of Africa to markets worldwide. We strive to be industry leaders by setting the standard for quality, freshness, and reliability. Our dedication to quality motivates us to build solid relationships with our farmers and consumers, bringing people together around a shared passion for premium herbs.

Our Farm

The farm is located in Kitengela, Kajiado County, Kenya. The areaâ€™s rich soil is ideal for growing a variety of produce, including herbs. In just three years, we have expanded from our initial setup of six greenhouse tunnels to more than fifty. While our primary crop is Basil, we also cultivate perennial crops such as Thyme, Sage, Sweet Marjoram, Parsley, Oregano, Sorrel, Lovage, Rosemary, Lemon Balm and Tarragon to broaden our product offering. We engage in sustainable farming practices such as use of drip method for irrigation and fertigation, and we use solar energy to pump water. More than 70% of our 50+ workforce consist of women, while more than 90% consist of youth.
